Data Analysis Project: British Airways Review

A Comprehensive Review of British Airways' Customer Satisfaction and Performance

Project Overview

This project utilizes a rich dataset from Kaggle, detailing customer reviews of British Airways, to analyze various factors affecting customer satisfaction. The dataset spans thousands of customer feedback entries, each containing valuable insights on flight experience, service quality, and more.

Methodology & Approach

To understand customer sentiment, the analysis focused on three key areas:

  • Customer Sentiment Analysis: Using natural language processing (NLP) to categorize reviews into positive, neutral, and negative sentiments.
  • Performance Indicators: Exploring metrics like on-time performance, cancellations, and customer complaints.
  • Data Visualization: Creating a comprehensive Tableau dashboard to display the insights effectively.

Data Visualization

The dashboard below presents the results of the analysis, including key performance indicators, sentiment trends, and customer satisfaction insights. The interactive dashboard allows users to explore the data and uncover patterns on their own.

Key Findings

From the analysis, we observed several important trends:

  • Sentiment Analysis: Over 60% of reviews expressed positive sentiment, highlighting favorable opinions of British Airways' in-flight service and customer care.
  • Punctuality: On-time performance was a significant factor, with flights that were punctual receiving higher ratings from passengers.
  • Frequent Complaints: A recurring theme in the negative reviews was delays and poor communication regarding cancellations.

Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can provide actionable insights for improving customer satisfaction in the airline industry. By leveraging customer reviews and performance data, British Airways can focus on the areas that matter most to their customers.
